Enable users to search company databases, analyze employee headcount trends, track hiring and attrition rates, explore corporate structures, and benchmark workforce metrics across industries. You can use the Aura connector to do: Investment Due Diligence: "Analyze Salesforce’s workforce growth over the past 12 months. Break down their employee distribution by department and compare their hiring velocity to other major SaaS companies like Hub Spot and Zendesk." ‍

<!-- para:0006 -->
Competitive Sales Intelligence: “Find detailed information about JPMorgan Chase, including their subsidiary structure, current workforce size, and recent hiring trends in their technology divisions.” ‍

<!-- para:0007 -->
Talent Market Analysis: “Compare employee growth rates and attrition patterns between Google, Meta, and Amazon over the last 6 months, focusing on engineering and product management functions.”
