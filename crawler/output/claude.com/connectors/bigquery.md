Integrate analytical data into your agent workflows to achieve superior business results. By connecting your agents with Big Query, you can provide actionable insights directly to them. Move past standard analytics and utilize Big Query's advanced features, such as forecasting, to generate higher-value intelligence.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Google Cloud Big Query to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**List Datasets: ** "Show me all datasets in my Big Query project"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Query Data: ** "Run a SQL query to analyze customer behavior trends"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Explore Tables: ** "What columns are available in the sales_data table?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Generate Insights: ** "Create a forecast based on historical revenue data"**
