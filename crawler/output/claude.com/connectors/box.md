<!-- para:0005 -->
Ask questions about your enterprise content, get insights from unstructured data, automate content workflows, and access other features from the Box platform. You can also search for files, preview documents inline, and extract insights—all without leaving your conversation. You can use Box in Claude to: Perform filesystem operations: "List all files in my Marketing folder and show me the most recently modified documents"

<!-- para:0006 -->
Use Box AI Agents: "Use Box AI to summarize the key insights from our Q4 sales report and extract action items"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Extract metadata from unstructured content: "Extract metadata from all contracts in the Legal folder and create a structured table with client names, dates, and contract values"
