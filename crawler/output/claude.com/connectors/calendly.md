Schedule smarter with Calendly in Claude. Manage your entire scheduling workflow through simple conversation — create and update event types, share scheduling links, adjust availability, book meetings, and more, without ever leaving Claude.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Calendly to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Create a scheduled event: ** "Book a 30 min coaching session with User A ([[email protected]](https://claude.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)) for this Thursday at 2 PM."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Find available time slots: ** "Find open slots next week for my 30 min coaching session."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Update availability: ** "Remove Fridays as available days for scheduling 30 min coaching sessions."**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Generate scheduling links: ** "Generate a one-time scheduling link for my 30 min coaching session."**
