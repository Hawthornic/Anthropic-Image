Browse, summarize, autofill, and even generate new Canva designs directly from Claude. Make Canva a native part of your workflow—helping you create polished visuals faster, with less friction. You can also create presentation outlines and customize branding and design in real-time to produce client-ready decks.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Canva in Claude to: ‍ Browse, Search & Summarize: "Summarize my Q2 product strategy doc"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Create New Designs from Conversation: "Generate a pitch deck for our AI launch with 5 slides and a bold tone"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Autofill Charts: "Add a chart showing monthly signups in NZ for Q1"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Autofill Brand Templates: "Populate our branded template with content for a product launch presentation, 8 slides, professional tone"

<!-- para:0010 -->
Import Files via Link: "Import this PDF [insert URL] into Canva"

<!-- para:0011 -->
Resize or Export: "Resize my Instagram post for Linked In and export as a PNG"

<!-- para:0012 -->
Create on-brand Canva presentations from an outline or brief.

<!-- para:0013 -->
Translate all text in a Canva design to another language, creating a translated copy.

<!-- para:0014 -->
Resize a Canva design into multiple social media formats (Facebook post or story, Instagram post or story, Linked In post) and export all as PNGs.
