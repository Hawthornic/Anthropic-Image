The Chronograph connector allows users to query portfolio data, analyze investments, search for entities, retrieve performance metrics, and access Chronograph's help documentation. The connector is designed for private investment workflows, providing programmatic access to portfolio analytics and reporting data.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Chronograph connector to do: Portfolio Exposure Analysis: "What are my top exposures to Health Care within the Client A portfolio?"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Sector-Based Fund Search: "Which of my funds have exposure to Consumer Finance?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Fund Performance Analysis: "What is the performance track record of Huron Oak Equity V?"
