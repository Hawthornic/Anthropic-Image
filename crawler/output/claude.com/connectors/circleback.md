# Circleback

<!-- para:0002 -->
Search and access context from meetings

<!-- para:0005 -->
Bring context from Circleback into Claude. Search and access meeting notes, transcripts, calendar events, emails, people, companies, and more.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Circleback connector to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
Find and reference past conversations: "When did we last meet with someone at Initech?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Get answers using context from your meetings: "Which customers requested export to PDF?"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Prepare for meetings: "Help me prepare for my next meeting with Ali"

<!-- para:0010 -->
Get up to speed on a relationship: "Summarize my recent emails and meetings with Initech"

<!-- para:0011 -->
Search calendar events: "What meetings do I have scheduled with the sales team this week?"

<!-- para:0012 -->
Find people and companies: "Show me details about contacts from Acme Corp"
