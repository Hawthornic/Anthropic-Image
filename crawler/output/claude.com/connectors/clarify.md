Retrieve, create, and update records using natural language. Analyze your sales pipeline, create tasks and lists automatically, and generate insights—all through conversation. Query deals, companies, people, and meetings without switching apps. Your Clarify data is retrieved in real-time, and permissions are respected. Stop clicking through interfaces. Just ask what you need.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Clarify to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Query your pipeline: ** "Show me all deals closing this month over $25k"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Prep for meetings: ** "I'm meeting with Acme Corp tomorrow—tell me everything about them and our open deals"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Analyze performance: ** "What's my win rate this quarter vs last quarter?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Create records: ** "Create a new company called Rocket Labs in the aerospace industry with 50 employees"**

<!-- para:0011 -->
**Update records: ** "Mark the Acme Corp deal as closed-won with final value of $50k"**

<!-- para:0012 -->
**Create and update lists: ** "Create a list of contacts at companies with more than 500 employees in San Francisco"**
