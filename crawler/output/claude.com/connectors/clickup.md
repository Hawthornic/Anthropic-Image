The Click Up connector enables secure, real-time interaction between AI agents like Claude and your Click Up workspace. Unlock powerful AI-driven automation by connecting your favorite AI tools directly to Click Up tasks, docs, Chat, and more.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Click Up connector to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
Find workspace members: "List all people in the workspace"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Create and organize tasks: "Create a task 'Add logout button' in Web App → Sprint Backlog"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Update task details: "Add 'High' priority to 'Add logout button' task"

<!-- para:0010 -->
Track time automatically: "Start tracking time on the current task I'm working on"

<!-- para:0011 -->
Manage documents: " Create a new document page for our Q1 planning notes"

<!-- para:0012 -->
Send team messages: "Send a message in the Engineering chat channel about the deployment schedule"
