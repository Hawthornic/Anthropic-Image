The Clinical Trials connector gives Claude access to Clinical Trials.gov, the NIH/NLM registry of FDA-regulated clinical studies conducted worldwide. Search for trials by condition, eligibility criteria, sponsor, or investigator. Analyze endpoints, find recruiting studies, and identify experienced research sites for clinical development planning.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Clinical Trials connector to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Match a Patient to Trials: **"I have a 62-year-old female patient with metastatic breast cancer in New York. Are there any recruiting trials she might qualify for?"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Gather Competitive Intelligence: **"What immunotherapy trials is Bristol Myers Squibb currently running?"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Support Site Selection: **"I'm planning a diabetes trial. Who are the most experienced investigators in Los Angeles?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Analyze Trial Endpoints: **"What primary endpoints are being used in current Phase 3 Alzheimer's trials?"**
