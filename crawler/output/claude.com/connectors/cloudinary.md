<!-- para:0005 -->
The Cloudinary connector enables you to upload, manage, transform, and analyze your media assets.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Cloudinary connector to: Upload media: "Upload an image/video from <URL/local file> to my Cloudinary account"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Organize assets: "Find all assets with tag=cat and move them to the folder named 'cats'"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Create platform-specific variations: "Create 3 variations of this image so it would fit Instagram story, Instagram post and Twitter, add my company logo as a watermark to those images"
