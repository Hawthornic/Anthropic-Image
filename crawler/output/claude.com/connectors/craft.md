Create beautifully structured documents, manage tasks, and build a personal knowledge base - all directly from Claude. Give Claude a persistent workspace to save research, draft content, track to-dos, and organize everything into folders, so your best thinking lives beyond the conversation.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Craft to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Search & Summarize Your Notes: ** "Find my notes about [topic] and summarize the key points"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Create & Organize Documents: ** "Create a new document in [folder] with sections for [what you need] "**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Manage Tasks & Review Your Week: ** "Add a task to tomorrow's daily note" or "Summarize what I got done this week from my daily notes"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Build Personal Databases: ** "Create a collection with columns for [fields] and add these entries"**
