<!-- para:0005 -->
Databricks offers two flavors of MCP servers: Managed servers and custom servers. • Managed servers: Databricks has ready-to-use servers that let agents query data and access tools in Unity Catalog. Unity Catalog permissions are always enforced, so agents and users can only access the tools and data they're allowed to. • Custom servers: Securely host your own MCP server as a Databricks app to bring your own server or run a third-party server.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Databricks connector to: Build customer support agents: "Help me assist a customer by looking up their account and plan information from our structured data, finding relevant support articles from our knowledge base, and calculating their current billing status"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Query structured data with Genie: "What are the top 10 customers by revenue in the telecommunications vertical?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Search unstructured data with Vector Search: "Find all support articles related to network outage troubleshooting"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Execute deterministic functions: "Calculate the prorated billing amount for a customer who upgraded their plan mid-cycle"
