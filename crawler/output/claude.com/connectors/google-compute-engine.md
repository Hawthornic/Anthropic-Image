<!-- para:0005 -->
Enables you to agentically manage your cloud compute infrastructure spanning deployment and operations.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Google Compute Engine to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Change machine type: ** "For 'instance1' in project 'project-1', apply the 'n2-standard-2' machine type now and tell me when it's back online"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Reset VM and verify accelerator attachment: ** "My training job on 'gpu-vm-1' in project 'project-1' froze. Reset it and confirm it still has its 4 T4 GPUs"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Create VM with centos: ** "Create a VM 'instance-1' in zone 'us-central1a' in project 'project-1' with image family 'centos-stream-10' and image project 'centos-cloud'"**
