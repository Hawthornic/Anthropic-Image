# Granola

<!-- para:0002 -->
The AI notepad for meetings

<!-- para:0005 -->
Connect Claude to your meeting history. Ask questions about your work and use your conversation context to get things done.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Granola to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Search across meetings: ** "Find all meetings where we discussed the Q1 product roadmap"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Review meeting details: ** "Show me the transcript from my last standup with the engineering team"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Get contextual answers: ** "What action items were assigned to me across this week's meetings?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**List recent meetings: ** "List all my meetings from the past week and summarize the key decisions"**
