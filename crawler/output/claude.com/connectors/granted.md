Search virtually every grant opportunity in existence - federal, state, and private foundation - using natural language. Most databases cover the big programs. Granted also surfaces the long tail: niche state grants, small family foundations, and hyper-local opportunities that nonprofits never find because no single database indexes them all. Research 133,000 US foundations with IRS 990 financials and key officers. Analyze 7 million federal award records to see who's actually winning.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Granted to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Discover Grants by Need: ** "I run a small nonprofit and want to start a mobile health clinic for pregnant women in a rural area. What grants can I apply for?"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Research Funders: ** "Tell me everything about the Gates Foundation - financials, recent grants, key people, and how to apply."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Analyze Competition: ** "We're applying for a Department of Energy grant. Who's actually winning these awards?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Find Niche Local Funding: ** "Our community has no grocery store within 10 miles. We want to build a community kitchen and urban farm. What funding exists?"**

<!-- para:0011 -->
**Search by State: ** "I run an after-school music program for kids in Mississippi. Are there grants for youth arts in our state?"**
