Connect Claude to your Gusto account to query and analyze your payroll and people data. Ask questions in natural language, build custom reports, and create charts to uncover trends and insights—no exports or tool-switching required. Eligible customers can also run payroll in Claude.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Gusto to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Analyze payroll trends: ** "Build a dashboard breaking down my last 6 payrolls" or "What was my tax liability on my most recent payroll?"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Review people data: ** "Show me employees with birthdays coming up next month"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Run payroll (eligible customers): ** "Run my next payroll"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Get company insights: ** "What are our department-wise compensation trends?"**
