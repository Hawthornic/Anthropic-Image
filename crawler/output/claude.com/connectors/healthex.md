<!-- para:0005 -->
The Health Ex connector connects Claude to your health records, securely pulling data from all your care providers into one place. Ask Claude to summarize your health, understand lab results, help prep for your next doctor's visit, and guide your daily routine.

<!-- para:0006 -->
Setup takes minutes: Verify your identity using biometrics and a government ID to ensure only you are getting access to your own health data, then connect your healthcare portal logins.

<!-- para:0007 -->
You can use the Health Ex connector to: **Get an Overview: **"Show me a summary of my health records."

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Learn About Your Health: **"Help me understand my most recent lab results."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Plan Your Care: **"What questions should I ask my doctor at my next visit?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Guide Your Daily Routine: **"What types of exercise would be best for me?"**

<!-- para:0011 -->
Health Ex is only available in the United States on pro and max Claude accounts. See guide for connecting to Health Ex [here](http://claude.com/resources/tutorials/using-the-healthex-connector-in-claude).

<!-- para:0012 -->
Health Ex is a Patient-First Privacy platform that follows the HIPAA Privacy Rule and is SOC2 compliant. Anthropic does not train its models on your Health Ex data.
