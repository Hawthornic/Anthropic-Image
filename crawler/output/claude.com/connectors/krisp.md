Connect your meeting history to AI tools so past decisions, action items, and conversation context are easy to find and use.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Krisp to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Search Meeting History: ** "Find all meetings where we discussed the Q4 budget"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Track Action Items: ** "Show me all pending action items from last week's meetings"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Get Meeting Context: ** "What decisions were made in yesterday's product planning meeting?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Upcoming Meeting Prep: ** "What should I prepare for my 3pm meeting with the marketing team?"**
