AI-powered document review meets attorney guidance. Connect to Legal Zoom for real-time scans of legal documents to identify critical risks and important clauses, advise on when to engage an attorney, and share notes in advance—all within one workflow. When matters require professional expertise, users will be routed directly into Legal Zoom's network of vetted attorneys for timely, jurisdiction-specific guidance.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Legal Zoom for:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Contract & Document Review: ** "Scan this service agreement for critical risks and important clauses, then advise if I need attorney review."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Business Formation: ** "Help me understand the legal requirements for forming an LLC in California and connect me with an attorney."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Employment Law: ** "Review this employment contract and flag any concerning clauses about non-compete agreements."**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Estate Planning: ** "I need guidance on creating a will - what are the key considerations and when should I involve an attorney?"**
