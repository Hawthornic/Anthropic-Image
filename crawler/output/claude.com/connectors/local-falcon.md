Local Falcon gives Claude access to real-time local search data across Google Maps, Apple Maps, and AI search platforms. With 37 tools spanning geo-grid rank tracking, competitor intelligence, campaign management, Google Business Profile monitoring, and AI search visibility tracking, Claude becomes a full-service local search and AI visibility analyst.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Local Falcon to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Local Search Rank Tracking: ** "Show me my latest scan for Holbrook House in SF"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**AI Search Visibility Analysis: ** "How visible is my restaurant location in AI search results for 'healthy food options near me'?"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Competitive Intelligence: ** "Compare my Google Maps rankings for Holbrook House against their top 3 competitors"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Google Business Profile Monitoring: ** "Check if Holbrook House has had changes to their business hours this week"**

<!-- para:0011 -->
**Credit & Campaign Management: ** "How many Local Falcon scan credits do I have left? Also, can you show me all scheduled scans?"**
