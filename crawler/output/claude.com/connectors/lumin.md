<!-- para:0005 -->
Connect Claude to Lumin PDF's document management services. Manage documents, send signature requests, retrieve workspace details, and convert Markdown to PDF through natural-language commands.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Lumin to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Document Management: ** "Upload this contract to my Lumin workspace and organize it in the legal documents folder."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Signature Requests: ** "Send this NDA to [[email protected]](https://claude.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) for digital signature and notify me when it's completed."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Workspace Organization: ** "Show me all documents in my shared workspace and their current status."**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**PDF Creation: ** "Convert this markdown document into a professional PDF and upload it to my workspace."**
