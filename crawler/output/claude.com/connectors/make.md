# Make

<!-- para:0002 -->
Run Make scenarios and manage your Make account

Run active and on-demand Make scenarios, view and modify Make scenarios and their related entities (e.g., connections, webhooks, and data stores), and view and modify teams and organizations - all from within Claude.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Make to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Run Scenarios: ** "Execute my customer onboarding automation"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Manage Workflows: ** "Show me all active scenarios in my account"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Configure Connections: ** "Update the Slack connection in my lead processing scenario"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Monitor Data: ** "Check the status of my CRM synchronization workflow"**
