<!-- para:0005 -->
The Medidata connector provides two tools for Medidata users: Platform Help and Predictive Site Ranking. Platform Help allows users to query Medidata's platform documentation. Predictive Site Ranking allows users to rank candidate study sites based on indication, past performance, and a variety of contexts.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Medidata connector to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
Get platform documentation answers: "How do I create a derived dataset in Data Connect?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Configure clinical data tools: "How do I configure edit checks for Rave EDC?"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Research product updates: "When was the last release of Clinical Data Studio and what features did it include?"

<!-- para:0010 -->
Integrate platform guidance with internal policies: "We need to import external lab data into Data Connect. How do I do this and ensure alignment with our SOPs?"

<!-- para:0011 -->
Predict top-performing clinical trial sites: "What are the top 10 predicted high performing sites for Phase 3 oncology trials?"

<!-- para:0012 -->
Analyze site geographical distribution: "What is the geographical distribution of the top performing sites by country for indication X?"
