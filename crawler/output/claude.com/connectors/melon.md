Explore a variety of Melon charts, get personalized music recommendations, and discover your listening habits—all in one place. Browse detailed information about artists, albums, and tracks. Search for songs and playlists using Melon charts and trending keywords. Enjoy personalized recommendations based on the songs you love and listen to most. You can even play songs directly in the Melon app.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Melon connector to: Play curated playlists: "Play some good workout songs on Melon"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Review listening history: "What songs did I play on Melon yesterday?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Get personalized recommendations: "Can you recommend some songs based on what I've been listening to lately?" ‍ The service is currently available in Korean only.
