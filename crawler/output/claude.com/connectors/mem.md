Give Claude the full context of your second brain by connecting your Mem knowledge base. Search your AI notebook for context, capture and recall ideas, save chats into new notes, edit and update living docs, and organize your AI workspace — simply by asking Claude. Use for: synthesizing and pulling actions items from meeting notes, deep research, personal knowledge management (PKM), knowledge repository building, task management, prioritization, and content development.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Mem to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Research validation: ** "Search my notes for pricing strategy and validate our thinking with research."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Content capture: ** "Create a note from this chat and add it to our GTM collection."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Task management: ** "List all open tasks from this week's notes and add to my to-do list."**
