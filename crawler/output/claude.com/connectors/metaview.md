<!-- para:0002 -->
The AI platform for recruiting.

<!-- para:0005 -->
Bring Metaview's hiring intelligence into Claude. Search, analyze, and extract insights from your recruiting data. Query conversations, source candidates, and start outreach. Use Claude alongside Metaview's AI agents to turn every conversation and signal into faster, better hiring decisions.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Metaview to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Interviewer Performance Coaching: ** "Give me feedback on my performance in my last interview — positives and negatives on how I interviewed the candidate"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Recruiting Analytics: ** "What are the average compensation expectations for all Account Executive candidates?"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Candidate Sourcing: ** "Find me candidates with startup and big tech experience, based in London."**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Outreach Management: ** "Add all qualified candidates from my backend engineer search to my outreach sequence."**
