Access Share Point, One Drive, Outlook, and Teams context directly in Claude. Search and analyze documents across sites and libraries, review email threads and communications, and gather insights from calendar and chat. Make Microsoft 365 a native part of your Claude workflows while respecting all existing permissions and security controls. You can use the Microsoft 365 connector to: Search documents across Share Point and One Drive: "Find all project proposals in our Share Point site from the last quarter and summarize the key themes"

<!-- para:0006 -->
Analyze email communications: "Review the email thread with [[email protected]](https://claude.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#06656a6f63687246637e676b766a632865696b) about the product launch and summarize outstanding action items"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Get calendar insights: "What meetings do I have this week related to the Q4 budget planning?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Review Teams conversations: "Summarize the key decisions from yesterday's Teams chat in the #product-launch channel"
