Connect Claude to a database of case law. With the Midpage MCP, Claude can conduct complex legal research, review opinions, and craft high quality work product. Everything is hyperlinked to real sources for easy verification.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Midpage Legal Research to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Conduct Legal Research: ** "Can a federal court compel arbitration if the arbitration is to occur outside of its district? 9th Circuit."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Draft Briefs: ** "Review the attached motion to dismiss and draft an opposition brief."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Find Key Cases: ** "I need cases from the third circuit court of appeals where the court granted a third-party's motion to quash a subpoena because it was too broad."**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Draft Memos: ** "Write a short memo on how the choice-of-law framework has evolved under Michigan law in the past 20 years."**
