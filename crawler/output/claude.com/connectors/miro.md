Access your board content and create content inside Miro from Claude. Use your teams workshops, technical specs and more in your AI workflows.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Miro to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Context to Code** Access your teams technical specifications and documentation to help build the right solutions with your AI Assistants.**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Visualize & Document your codebase** Visualize and document your code base by leveraging your AI Agent to understand your codebase and visualize it in both documents and diagrams on Miro, where you can now share it with stakeholders.**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Summarize workshop** Summarize your teams workshop, extract next steps for your team to tackle. For example, after a retro you want to extract what are the next steps.**
