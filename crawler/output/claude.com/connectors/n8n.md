# n8n

<!-- para:0002 -->
Access and run your n8n workflows

Talk to your n8n instance and trigger automations - search for workflows, get their details, and execute them directly from Claude. You can use n8n connector to: List all your workflows: “What workflows do I have on my n8n instance?” Search for a specific action: “Do I have an automation for creating and publishing posts on my social media handles?” Execute your workflows: "Ask my n8n personal assistant to give me a summary of my meetings from yesterday" "Check my calendar and book me in for my tennis classes on Friday afternoon"
