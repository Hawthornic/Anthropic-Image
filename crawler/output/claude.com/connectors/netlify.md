Create, deploy, and manage websites on Netlify. Control all aspects of your site from creating secrets to enforcing access controls to aggregating your form submissions. The full power of the Netlify platform available from Claude.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Netlify connector to: Create and deploy sites: "Create a new site on my Netlify team and deploy it"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Manage environment variables: "Create an environment variable called 'MY_API_KEY' and set it to the value 'hello, world'"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Configure access controls: "Turn on password protection for my site and set it to 'youshallnotpass'"
