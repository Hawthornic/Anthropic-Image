Manage incidents, create services, schedule overrides, and track on-call rotations with your Pager Duty account

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Pager Duty to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Incident Management: ** "Create a new incident for the database outage and assign it to the infrastructure team"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**On-call Scheduling: ** "Create a schedule override for this weekend and put Sarah on-call instead of John"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Team Management: ** "Add the new Dev Ops engineer to the infrastructure response team"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Status Updates: ** "Add a note to the current incident explaining the root cause and resolution steps"**
