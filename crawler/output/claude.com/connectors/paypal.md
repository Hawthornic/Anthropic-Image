Interact with Pay Pal’s payment ecosystem to process transactions, manage subscriptions, handle disputes, access customer payment data, generate financial reports, and monitor payment flows. Enable secure payment operations and business insights through conversational commands for comprehensive payment management.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Pay Pal connector to: Create invoices: "Create an invoice for {customer_email} including 2 hours of consulting at $100 per hour"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Get transaction reports: "Get the list of my transactions for last 30 days"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Create products: "Create a new product with the name '{product_name}' of type DIGITAL"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Create subscription plans: "Create a Pay Pal subscription plan for product '{product_name}' with billing cycle '{billing_cycle}', price '{price} {currency}'. Set trial period cycle to '{trial_period}'"

<!-- para:0010 -->
Set up subscriptions: "Create a Pay Pal subscription for the 'Premium Membership' plan and subscribe [[email protected]](https://claude.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#e2819791968d8f8790a2878f838b8ecc818d8f)"
