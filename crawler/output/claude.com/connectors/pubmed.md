<!-- para:0005 -->
Provides access to Pub Med's biomedical citations and Pub Med Central's full-text archive. Search articles, retrieve metadata and abstracts, access full-text content (when available in PMC), find related research, and more.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Pub Med connector to: Search recent publications: "How many cancer research articles have been published this month?"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Summarize research papers: "Summarize the research paper 'Nucleoporin-mediated regulation of cell identity genes'"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Query scientific mechanisms: "What is known about the mechanisms of DNA repair including the roles of Rad51 and Brca1 in Pub Med?"
