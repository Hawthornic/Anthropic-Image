<!-- para:0005 -->
Access structured first-party IR data from over 14,500+ public companies across 65 markets Quartr delivers live and recorded earnings calls, real-time and historical transcripts with speaker identification, slide presentations, filings, reports, and event summaries. Best-in-class reliability and timeliness. Ideal for financial research, investment analysis, and building data-driven workflows on top of institutional-grade public company data.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Quartr for:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Earnings call analysis: ** "List forward-looking statements for NVIDIA from recent transcripts and filings that reference expected progress or results in the upcoming earnings release."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Competitive intelligence: ** "Compare how semiconductor companies talk about the same end markets and demand."**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**KPI tracking: ** "What KPIs has Spotify introduced, stopped emphasizing, or reframed across its last eight quarters?"**

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Narrative assessment: ** "What were the most recurring themes in analyst questions during Eli Lilly's last two earnings calls? Highlight key areas of focus and concern."**
