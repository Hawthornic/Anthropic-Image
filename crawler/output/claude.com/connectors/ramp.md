# Ramp

<!-- para:0002 -->
Search, access, and analyze your Ramp financial data

<!-- para:0003 -->
- Category: Financial services
- Used in: [Claude](https://claude.ai/directory/61bac03c-3f98-4b3c-affb-1b99533fa82c) [Claude desktop app](https://claude.com/connectors/ramp#) [Claude mobile app](https://claude.com/connectors/ramp#) [Claude Code](https://claude.com/connectors/ramp#) [Claude Code](https://claude.com/connectors/ramp#) [Claude API](https://claude.com/connectors/ramp#) [Claude API](https://claude.com/connectors/ramp#)

<!-- para:0005 -->
Enables secure, read-only access to Ramp data for customer-defined logic. Supports loading and querying transactions, reimbursements, bills, purchase orders, cards, vendors, and more, enabling dynamic data fetching and analysis.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use the Ramp connector to: Analyze vendor spending: "Show me our top 10 vendors by spend last quarter"

<!-- para:0007 -->
Monitor department budgets: "Which departments exceeded their budgets last month?"

<!-- para:0008 -->
Review travel expenses: "Analyze our travel expenses by employee and location"

<!-- para:0009 -->
Track pending bills: "Find all pending bills over $10,000"

<!-- para:0010 -->
Compare spending trends: "Compare this month's spending to the same period last year"

<!-- para:0011 -->
Analyzes vendor spend data from Ramp and exports to connected systems.
