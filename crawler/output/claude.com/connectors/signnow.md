<!-- para:0005 -->
Get documents signed faster without switching between tools. The Sign Now MCP server connects Claude directly to your Sign Now account, letting you manage the entire e Signature workflow through conversation. Send signature requests, create documents from templates and pre-fill them, track invite statuses, and retrieve signed files — all by describing what you need in plain language. Whether you're handling contracts, agreements, or approvals, Claude handles the Sign Now actions so you can focus on your work instead of chasing signatures.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Sign Now to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Create and Send Documents: ** "Create a document from the Employee NDA template and send it to [[email protected]](https://claude.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) for signature"**

<!-- para:0008 -->

<!-- para:0009 -->

<!-- para:0010 -->
**Download Signed Documents: ** "Get the download link for the completed Employment Contract"**
