<!-- para:0005 -->
The Starburst MCP server exposes a secure, read-only SQL tool for Claude. It enables governed data discovery, lightweight sampling, and execution of analytical queries across Starburst catalogs, while enforcing existing permissions, timeouts, and result-size limits and returning compact, structured results for effective AI agent workflows.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Starburst to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Discover available data: ** "What catalogs and schemas are available in my Starburst account?"**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Run analytical queries: ** "Query the sales table to show total revenue by region for last quarter"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Sample data for exploration: ** "Show me a sample of 10 rows from the customers table"**
