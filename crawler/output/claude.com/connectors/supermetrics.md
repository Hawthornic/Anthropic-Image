<!-- para:0005 -->
Supermetrics MCP Server connects Claude to the 200+ marketing platforms so you can find, pull, and act on marketing, sales, and analytics data through natural language. It's great for quick performance answers, diagnosing problem situations, and turning questions into tables, summaries or visualizations.

<!-- para:0006 -->
You can use Supermetrics to:

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Summarize marketing performance: ** "Summarize last week's performance across Google Ads, Meta, and Linked In: spend, revenue, ROAS, CPA, and top 5 campaigns."**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Anomaly detection: ** "Looking at last week's campaign performance, highlight any anomalies and explain likely causes"**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Ad improvements: ** "Based on campaign performance data, suggest optimizations (budget shifts, audiences, creatives) and explain why"**
