## The challenge of transforming technical recruitment

<!-- para:0006 -->
Traditional recruiting methods have reached their breaking point. Companies feel pressure to hire exceptional talent while drowning in inefficient processes. Resumes alone cannot determine interview eligibility, while manual screening calls consume countless recruiter hours. Take-home tests fare no better—with up to 85% of candidates using AI to complete exercises, these assessments provide little real insight into abilities.

<!-- para:0007 -->
The cost is also staggering. Companies like Legal Soft previously needed 33 staff members just to handle screening interviews. This time-intensive process often fails to identify the best candidates, with qualified applicants overlooked because recruiters lack the bandwidth to properly evaluate everyone.

<!-- para:0008 -->
micro1 recognized that solving this challenge required more than incremental improvements. They aim to make every recruiter on the planet 100x more powerful through AI-powered interviewing. This means reimagining the entire technical assessment process to create a scalable system that maintains the high standards companies require for technical hires.

<!-- para:0019 -->
