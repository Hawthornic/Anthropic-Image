# Common Room

<!-- para:0002 -->
Turn Common Room into your GTM copilot with account research, call prep, and personalized outreach grounded in real signal data

<!-- para:0003 -->
- Anthropic Verified
- Install in [Claude Cowork](https://claude.ai/desktop/customize/plugins/new?marketplace=anthropics/knowledge-work-plugins&plugin=common-room)
- Made by: [Common Room](https://commonroom.io/)
- Installs

<!-- para:0004 -->
Turn Common Room into your GTM copilot. Research accounts and contacts, prep for calls with attendee profiles and talking points, and draft personalized outreach across email, Linked In, and phone. Build targeted prospect lists, generate weekly briefings for every upcoming call, and create strategic account plans — all grounded in real signal data from product usage, engagement and intent signals, so every output reflects what's actually happening in your accounts.

<!-- para:0005 -->
**Call Prep: ** Pull together a company snapshot, attendee profiles, recent signals, and talking points before any meeting.**

<!-- para:0006 -->
**Account & Contact Research: ** Look up any account or contact and get a clear picture — buying signals, activity history, scores, CRM data, and enrichment from RoomieAI and Spark.**

<!-- para:0007 -->
**Compose Outreach: ** Draft emails, call scripts, and Linked In messages grounded in a contact's real activity and signals.**

<!-- para:0008 -->
**Prospecting: ** Build prospect lists by industry, size, location, or signal activity — find net-new companies or surface warm contacts in existing accounts.**

<!-- para:0009 -->
**Weekly Prep Brief: ** Generate a briefing covering every external call in the next seven days — company context, attendee profiles, signal highlights, and recommended objectives.**
