# Search Fit SEO

<!-- para:0002 -->
Free AI SEO toolkit: audit, strategy, optimize, markup, keywords, visibility. Works everywhere.

<!-- para:0003 -->
- Install in [Claude Code](https://claude.com/plugins/searchfit-seo#)
- Made by: [Search Fit.ai](https://searchfit.ai/)
- Installs627

<!-- para:0004 -->
A comprehensive, free AI-powered SEO toolkit that brings professional-grade search optimization directly into Claude Code. Audit any website for technical and on-page SEO issues, plan content strategy, generate JSON-LD schema markup, cluster keywords by intent, detect broken links, optimize internal linking, and track AI visibility — all without leaving your editor.

<!-- para:0005 -->
The plugin includes 11 auto-activating skills (SEO audit, technical SEO, on-page optimization, broken link detection, internal linking, schema markup, content strategy, content briefs, keyword clustering, AI visibility tracking, and content translation) plus 3 autonomous agents: an SEO Auditor that produces scored audit reports, a Content Strategist that builds full content plans, and a Competitor Analyzer that identifies market opportunities.

<!-- para:0006 -->
**How to use: ** Run `/seo-check` for a quick scored SEO evaluation of any HTML page. Use `/generate-schema` to produce ready-to-paste JSON-LD structured data (supports Article, Product, FAQ, How To, and more). Try `/keyword-cluster` with a comma-separated keyword list to group them by intent and map to content types. Use `/create-topic` and `/create-content` to research topics and generate SEO-optimized articles, or `/translate-content` for multilingual localization. You can also ask naturally — e.g., "audit example.com for SEO issues" or "create a content strategy for my blog" — and the relevant skills will activate automatically.**
