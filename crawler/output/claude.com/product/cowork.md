# Delegate to Claude, delight in the result

<!-- para:0002 -->

<!-- para:0004 -->

## How it works

<!-- para:0009 -->
Find Cowork in your desktop app, alongside Chat and Code. Switch to Cowork when you want Claude to execute non-coding tasks.

<!-- para:0010 -->
### Describe what you need

<!-- para:0011 -->
Tell Claude what you want from your desktop or phone. Claude picks the fastest path: a connector for Slack, Chrome for web research, or your screen to open apps when there's no direct integration.

<!-- para:0012 -->
Claude does the work

<!-- para:0013 -->
Claude works through each step, looping you in before anything significant. Watch in real time or walk away. For recurring tasks, tell Claude once and it handles the rest.

<!-- para:0014 -->
You’re in control

<!-- para:0015 -->
You choose which folders and connectors Claude can access. Before Claude acts, it shows you the plan and waits for your approval. Redirect, refine, or take a different approach at any step.

## Stay in control

<!-- para:0030 -->
Claude Cowork is in research preview. Agent safety is still in development. Take precautions while using it.

<!-- para:0031 -->
### You decide what Claude can access

<!-- para:0032 -->
You choose which folders and connectors Claude can see and it can't access anything without your explicit permission. [Learn more](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/13364135-use-cowork-safely)

<!-- para:0033 -->
### Claude asks before acting

<!-- para:0034 -->
Always confirm before Claude handles financial, personal, or work-critical tasks.Before taking significant actions, Claude shows you what it plans to do and waits for your approval. You can redirect, refine, or take a different approach at any step.

<!-- para:0035 -->
### For Teams & Enterprise users

<!-- para:0036 -->
Cowork conversation history is stored locally on your device, not on Anthropic's servers. Enterprise features like audit logs, compliance API, data exports do not currently capture Cowork activity. Administrators can toggle it on or off at their discretion. Avoid granting access to local files with sensitive information, like financial documents.
