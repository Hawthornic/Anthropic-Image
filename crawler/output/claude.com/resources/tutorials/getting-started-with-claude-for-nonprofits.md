# Getting started with Claude for nonprofits

<!-- para:0002 -->
- Category: Nonprofits
- Product Claude.ai
- Reading time Watch time5minmin
- Share [Copy link](https://claude.com/resources/tutorials/getting-started-with-claude-for-nonprofits#)https://claude.com/resources/tutorials/getting-started-with-claude-for-nonprofits

<!-- para:0003 -->
Claude for nonprofits enhances mission-driven organizations with specialized capabilities, connecting to donor management platforms, foundation research databases, and collaboration tools while maintaining transparency and accountability. Learn more on the [nonprofit solutions page](https://claude.com/solutions/nonprofits).

<!-- para:0004 -->
## How to access nonprofit tools and features

<!-- para:0005 -->
Claude for nonprofits is available on two plans:

<!-- para:0006 -->
- **Team plan: ** $8/user/month
- **Enterprise plan: ** $10/user/month

<!-- para:0007 -->
Both plans include access to Claude Sonnet 4.5 and Haiku 4.5, with a minimum of 2 seats for teams under 150.

<!-- para:0008 -->
To get started, verify your nonprofit status through our partner Goodstack by completing [this short form](https://validate.poweredbypercent.com/anthropic). The verification process takes 2–3 minutes for Team plans. For Enterprise pricing, [contact our sales team](https://claude.com/contact-sales/nonprofits).

<!-- para:0009 -->
## Nonprofit operations and fundraising connectors

<!-- para:0010 -->
Claude connects to three nonprofit-specific connectors through the Model Context Protocol (MCP):

<!-- para:0011 -->
- [**Benevity**](https://benevity.com/)**: ** Access a database of 2.4M+ validated nonprofits for cause discovery and research
- [**Blackbaud**](https://www.blackbaud.com/)**: ** Connect to Raiser's Edge NXT for donor records, gift history, events, and personalized communications
- [**Candid**](https://candid.org/)**: ** Search 1.9M+ nonprofits and foundations with detailed profiles, financials, and grant data

<!-- para:0012 -->
Additional connectors include Microsoft 365, Gmail, Google Drive, Asana, Slack, and Hub Spot. Browse all available connectors in the [connector directory](https://claude.com/connectors).

## Frequently asked questions

<!-- para:0019 -->
#### Who is eligible?

<!-- para:0020 -->
501(c)(3) organizations, K–12 schools, and qualifying healthcare organizations.

<!-- para:0021 -->
#### Who is not eligible?

<!-- para:0022 -->
Government agencies, political campaigns, higher education institutions, and healthcare systems.

<!-- para:0023 -->
#### How does validation work?

<!-- para:0024 -->
We partner with [Goodstack](https://validate.poweredbypercent.com/anthropic) for nonprofit verification. Team plan validation takes 2–3 minutes. If you have issues with verification, [contact Goodstack](https://help.goodstack.org/hc/en-us).

<!-- para:0025 -->
#### How long does the discount last?

<!-- para:0026 -->
The nonprofit discount remains active as long as your organization maintains its nonprofit status.

<!-- para:0027 -->
#### What if I already have a full-priced account?

<!-- para:0028 -->
Go to [Admin settings > Billing](https://claude.ai/admin-settings/billing) to update your plan, or [reach out to our support team](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9015913-how-to-get-support).

<!-- para:0029 -->
#### Is my data used for training?

<!-- para:0030 -->
*Note: By default, Anthropic does not use data from Team or Enterprise accounts to train models.* Learn more at our [Privacy Center](https://privacy.claude.com/en/collections/10672411-data-handling-retention) and [Trust Center](https://trust.anthropic.com/).*

<!-- para:0031 -->
#### Can I get a tax exemption?

<!-- para:0032 -->
Submit your tax exemption certificate through [support](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9015913-how-to-get-support) to receive a refund and apply the exemption to future invoices.

<!-- para:0033 -->
#### What about organizations with fewer than five employees?

<!-- para:0034 -->
Individual users can access Claude Pro at $20/month. Explore the [AI fluency for nonprofits course](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-for-nonprofits) and [nonprofit use cases](https://claude.com/resources/use-cases/category/nonprofits) to get started.

<!-- para:0035 -->
#### What support and training is available?

<!-- para:0036 -->
Visit the [use case library](https://claude.com/resources/use-cases/category/nonprofits) and [help center](https://support.claude.com/en/) for resources and guidance.

## Troubleshooting

<!-- para:0038 -->
- **Discount link not working: ** Clear your browser cache and try again in an incognito window. If the issue persists, [contact support](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9015913-how-to-get-support).
- **Team plan option greyed out: ** Make sure you're signed in to the correct account and have admin permissions
- **Email domain questions: ** All team members don't need the same domain. Learn about [allowed email domains](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/13325567-account-management-faqs#h_b54c41c86c).
- **Connecting tools: ** Learn how to [browse and connect tools from the directory](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/11724452-using-the-connectors-directory-to-extend-claude-s-capabilities).
