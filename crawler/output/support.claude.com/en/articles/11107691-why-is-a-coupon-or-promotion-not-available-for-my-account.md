section: articles
date: 2026-03-16
---

<!-- para:0001 -->
Coupons and promotions are generally only available to users trying out a paid plan for the first time.

<!-- para:0002 -->
A "This promotion is not available for your account" error message means that you are ineligible for the promotion due to being either an active paid subscriber or having been a paid subscriber in the past.

<!-- para:0003 -->
Additionally, App Store subscribers (iOS or Android) are unable to take advantage of promotions at this time. If you are interested in transitioning from an App Store subscription to a direct subscription, you'll need to cancel and resubscribe once your current subscription term ends.
