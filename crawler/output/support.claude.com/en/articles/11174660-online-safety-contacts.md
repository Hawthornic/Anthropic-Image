## **Designated Point of Contact for Authorities**

<!-- para:0002 -->
Government authorities can contact Anthropic directly by emailing [regulator-inquiries@anthropic.com](mailto:regulator-inquiries@anthropic.com). This is reserved for government officials seeking a point of contact and for governmental submissions to remove content.

<!-- para:0003 -->
For other types of inquiries, please use the mechanisms described below. ￼

<!-- para:0004 -->
## **Illegal Content Reporting**

<!-- para:0005 -->
